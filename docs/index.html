Features

Why choose Messy?

The best Messenger desktop experience on macOS — no ads, no limits.

🔧 Session fix

Fixes the bug where you log in as account A but see account B because cookies/sessions were mixed between profiles.

🔓 Log out & log in again

Right-click an account → log out to wipe its session and sign in with a different account instantly. No need to delete the account.

🔒 App Lock

Protect your messages with a 4-digit PIN. Auto-locks when idle (1–30 minutes). Rate-limited after 5 wrong tries, keyboard support.

🔴 Per-account badge

Each account shows its own unread count on the sidebar. The combined badge is aggregated automatically onto the taskbar overlay.

Dual-sidebar layout

The left sidebar holds your accounts, the right sidebar holds the tools. Supports scrolling and mouse-drag when you have many accounts.

Smart navigation

Home and Back buttons make it easy to return to your messages when you wander off to a profile or group page.

Multi-account

Log in to multiple Messenger accounts at once. Each one is fully isolated (sandboxed partitions).

Privacy protection

Block "Seen" and "Typing" states. Read your messages completely anonymously, like J2TEAM Security.

Automatic updates (OTA)

Download and install new updates directly inside the app with a single click.

Dark/Light Mode

An elegant dark theme or a soft light theme. Switch quickly with the toolbar button.

Shortcuts & system tray

Ctrl+Shift+M shows/hides Messenger. Minimize to the system tray and keep running in the background.
